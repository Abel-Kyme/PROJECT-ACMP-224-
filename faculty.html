ABOUT OUR FACULTIES

ABOUT

Faculty of Science

Faculty of Science

Founded in 1952, the Faculty of Science has been a hub for innovation and discovery. It offers programs in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science. Alumni have gone on to pioneer research in renewable energy, AI, and biotechnology. Fun fact: the faculty once grew a plant species previously thought extinct in a controlled lab environment, setting a global record.

Faculty of Arts

Faculty of Arts

Established in 1948, the Faculty of Arts nurtures creativity and critical thinking through literature, history, languages, and philosophy. Fun fact: students once recreated an entire Shakespeare festival on campus, drawing audiences from across the country. Its library holds over 100,000 rare manuscripts dating back to the 18th century.

Faculty of Engineering

Faculty of Engineering

Founded in 1960, the Faculty of Engineering has been training innovators in civil, mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering. Fun fact: the faculty built the first solar-powered vehicle in the country in 1985, which still resides in the campus museum. Students often collaborate with tech startups to create solutions for smart cities.

Faculty of Medicine

Faculty of Medicine

Established in 1955, the Faculty of Medicine has produced some of the country's leading doctors and medical researchers. Fun fact: its first graduating class helped establish the first national blood bank. The faculty is also known for pioneering malaria research in the region, saving thousands of lives.

Faculty of Law

Faculty of Law

Established in 1950, the Faculty of Law has a rich history of shaping legal minds. Fun fact: its moot court team has won international competitions for three consecutive years. The faculty’s alumni have contributed to drafting national legislation and advocating human rights globally.

Faculty of Education

Faculty of Education

Founded in 1945, the Faculty of Education has trained generations of teachers. Fun fact: its alumni created the first nationwide literacy program, which increased literacy rates by 30% in the 1970s. The faculty emphasizes innovative teaching, curriculum design, and inclusive education.

Faculty of Agriculture

Faculty of Agriculture

Established in 1962, the Faculty of Agriculture trains students in modern farming, agribusiness, and sustainable practices. Fun fact: the faculty developed a drought-resistant crop variety adopted nationwide. Alumni lead research projects that improve food security in the region.
