The Dean of Students Office at Egerton University is commited to promoting welfare, holistic development, and academic success of students. The office provides essential support services that enhance the student experience and empower learners both inside and outside the classroom.

Our Mission

To promote holistic student development by providing effective welfare, counseling, mentorship, leadership training, and support services that enable students to thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in student support services, nurturing disciplined, responsible, and empowered graduates who positively contribute to society.

Mandate of the Dean of Students

  • Promote students’ physical, psychological, social, spiritual, and leadership development
  • Offer individual and group counseling services
  • Conduct first-year orientation programmes
  • Coordinate student welfare and discipline matters
  • Oversee sports and games activities
  • Provide career guidance, mentorship, and public sensitization
  • Facilitate student financial support and HELB liaison
